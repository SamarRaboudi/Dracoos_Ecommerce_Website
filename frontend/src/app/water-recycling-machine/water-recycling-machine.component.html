Details

Greywater is the wastewater that comes from baths, showers, sinks, and washing machines. It is not contaminated with fecal matter, so it can be safely reused for non-drinking purposes. The Dracoss Greywater Recycling Machine uses a variety of filtration and purification methods to remove contaminants from greywater, including:


  • Special Sediment Tank
  • Filtration with membranes
  • UV disinfection

The Dracoss Greywater Recycling Machine is a great way to save water and money, and it is also a great way to protect the environment. By recycling greywater, you can reduce the amount of wastewater that goes to the treatment plant, which can help to conserve water resources and reduce pollution.
